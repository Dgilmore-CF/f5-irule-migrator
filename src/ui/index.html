What this tool does

It applies pattern-matching heuristics to translate F5 BIG-IP iRules (TCL) and ASM policy exports (XML) into suggested Cloudflare Rules, WAF rules, Rate Limiting rules, and Snippets — alongside dashboard steps, API calls, and Terraform.

What this tool does NOT do

  • It does not guarantee functional equivalence with your F5 configuration.
  • It does not understand all F5 features. Complex logic will be flagged for manual review.
  • It does not connect to or modify your Cloudflare account.
  • It does not replace security review, change management, or QA.

Your responsibilities

  • Review every generated rule before deploying it. Heuristic conversions can be wrong, incomplete, or unsafe in ways that are not obvious from the output.
  • Test thoroughly in a non-production environment. Run synthetic traffic, replay logs, and validate WAF behavior against your real workloads before cutting traffic over.
  • Do not paste secrets (API tokens, private keys, cookies, customer data) into this tool. Inputs are processed in your browser session and a stateless Worker — but you are responsible for the data you send.
  • Comply with your own change-management and security review processes.
